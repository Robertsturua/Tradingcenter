Use case

Designed for consumer cross-checking and market awareness.

These resources can be used to cross-check pricing, symbols, news, and market conditions while investigating a trading entity. External links open in a new tab. The Trading Centre provides them as reference utilities commonly used by market participants.

Cross-check approach

Compare the entity’s claims against independent price history, basic market facts, and public reporting. Where a withdrawal dispute exists, review the Withdrawals page for clearance terminology used in communications.
