Directory overview

Directory entries summarise how entities present their services publicly, including brand identifiers, stated activities, and operational model keywords (e.g., execution-only brokerage, OTC routing, introducing broker structures). Entries may also include risk notes where withdrawal behavior and settlement routing have been reported as inconsistent.

How monitoring is applied

Monitoring compares declared operating models against observed transaction sequencing, withdrawal time-to-release, settlement chain behavior, and beneficiary pathway alignment.
